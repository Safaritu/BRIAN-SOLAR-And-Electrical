Frequently Asked Questions About Solar & Electrical Services

Get answers to common questions about our solar installation services, processes, and business model in Kenya.

Do you provide solar installation services across Kenya?

Yes, Brian Solar & Electrical operates as a service-area business and provides solar installation services across Kenya including Nairobi, Kiambu, Machakos, Kajiado, and surrounding counties. We bring all equipment and expertise directly to your location.

Do you install borehole solar systems?

Yes, we specialize in borehole solarization for residential, agricultural, and commercial use across Kenya. Our solar-powered water pumping systems can reduce water pumping costs by up to 90% compared to grid or diesel-powered systems.

Are your electrical installations safe and compliant with Kenyan standards?

Yes, all our electrical installations are done by qualified technicians following safety and compliance standards set by the Energy Regulatory Commission of Kenya. We ensure all work meets or exceeds local electrical codes and regulations.

How long does a typical solar installation take in Kenya?

Most residential solar installations are completed within 1-3 days, depending on the system size and complexity. Commercial installations may take longer based on the scale of the project. We provide a clear timeline during the initial consultation.

Do you offer warranties on your solar installations?

Yes. We provide workmanship warranties on our installations, and all equipment comes with manufacturer warranties. Our standard workmanship warranty covers installation for 2 years, while solar panel warranties typically range from 10-25 years depending on the brand.

Can you help with government approvals or permits for solar installations?

Yes. We assist with the necessary paperwork and approvals required for solar installations, including Energy Regulatory Commission (ERC) approvals where applicable. We guide you through the entire process to ensure compliance with Kenyan regulations.
