Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services, processes, and business model.

Do you have a physical office or showroom?

No. We operate as a service-area business and work directly at customer locations. This allows us to keep costs competitive while providing personalized service. We bring samples, equipment, and expertise directly to your site for assessment and consultation.

Do you supply solar equipment and components?

Yes. We source quality components from trusted suppliers and can provide everything needed for your solar or electrical project. We work with reputable brands and ensure all equipment comes with proper warranties and certifications.

How long does a typical solar installation take?

Most residential solar installations are completed within 1-3 days, depending on the system size and complexity. Commercial installations may take longer based on the scale of the project. We provide a clear timeline during the initial consultation.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes. We provide workmanship warranties on our installations, and all equipment comes with manufacturer warranties. Our standard workmanship warranty covers installation for 2 years, while equipment warranties vary by manufacturer (typically 5-25 years).

Can you help with government approvals or permits?

Yes. We assist with the necessary paperwork and approvals required for solar installations, including Energy Regulatory Commission (ERC) approvals where applicable. We guide you through the entire process to ensure compliance with local regulations.

What maintenance do solar systems require?

Solar systems require minimal maintenance, primarily regular cleaning of panels and periodic system checks. We recommend professional maintenance every 6-12 months to ensure optimal performance and early detection of any issues.
